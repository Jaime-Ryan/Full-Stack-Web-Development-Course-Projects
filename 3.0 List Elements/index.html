Angela's Cinnamon Roll Recipe

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minuites.
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours.
  6. After the dough has doubled in size, roll it out into a large rectangle.
  7. Melt the butter for the filling and mix in the sugar and cinnamon.
  8. Spread the filling onto the dough then roll the dough into a swiss roll.
  9. Cut the roll into 3cm sections and place flat into a baking tray.
  10. Pre-heat the oven to 350F or 180C, then bake the rolls for 20-25min until lightly brown.
  11. Let the rolls cool for 10 minutes before serving.
  12. Enjoy your delicious cinnamon rolls!
  13. Optional: Drizzle with icing or serve with cream cheese frosting.
  14. Store any leftovers in an airtight container for up to 3 days.
  15. For an extra touch, sprinkle some chopped nuts or raisins in the filling.
  16. Experiment with different spices like nutmeg or cardamom for a unique flavor.
  17. Pair with a cup of coffee or tea for a perfect breakfast or snack.
  18. Share with friends and family for a delightful treat.
  19. Consider making a double batch to freeze some for later.

  21. For a richer flavor, use whole milk instead of skim or low-fat.
  22. Try adding a pinch of salt to the dough for enhanced flavor.
  23. For a gooey center, add a bit more butter to the filling.
  24. Use a rolling pin to ensure even thickness when rolling out the dough.
  25. Let the dough rest for 5-10 minutes before rolling it out to make it easier to handle.
  26. For a fun twist, add chocolate chips or dried fruit to the filling.
